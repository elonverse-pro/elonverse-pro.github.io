What is Hybrid Blockchain?

Hybrid blockchain ek combination hota hai public aur private blockchain ka. Public blockchain transparency aur decentralization deta hai, jabki private blockchain control aur scalability provide karta hai. Hybrid model dono ke best features ko combine karta hai.

Is model me sensitive data private reh sakta hai jabki selected transactions public network par visible ho sakti hain. Ye structure enterprises aur DeFi projects dono ke liye beneficial hai.

Public vs Private vs Hybrid Blockchain

Public Blockchain: Fully decentralized, transparent, lekin scalability issue ho sakta hai.

Private Blockchain: Controlled access, high speed, lekin limited transparency.
